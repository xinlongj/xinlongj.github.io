Xinlong Jiang, Associate Professor

Dr.Xinlong Jiang graduated from the Institute of computing, Chinese Academy of Sciences, majoring in computer application, and is a public doctoral candidate in computer science, University of California, Irvine. Currently, he is deputy researcher of the computing system research center of CAS Computing Institute, executive director of the Joint Laboratory of Computing Institute semeskay vehicle network and vice president of the Institute of computing of CAS.

His research interests include ExplainAbel AI 、Federal Learning and Human–Computer-Interaction UBIQUITOUS-COMPUTE
