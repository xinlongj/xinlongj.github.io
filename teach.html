Artificial Intelligence Foundations

One of the compulsory courses of undergraduate students(junior students) in UCAS since Fall 2016. 

Course Overview

This course introduces foundations of artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on search algorithms, propositional logic and supervised learning algorithms and their applications. The recommended teaching material is “Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach, 3rd Edition”.
